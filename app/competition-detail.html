Reinventing Gwalior’s 16 Event

Deadline 26th July 2017
CASH PRIZE 50000
Participate in Competition Submit Design in Competition
  • Info

Brief

Gwalior Trade Fair is one of the oldest and largest trade fair in Madhya Pradesh, in Northern India. It was started in 1905 by the king of Gwalior : Maharaja Madhav Rao Scindia and with the history of 111 years, Gwalior Trade Fair is held at vast and modern fair complex spread over 104 acres. 5000 pavilions and shops, provided with ready-to use structures for showrooms and exhibitions. The fair complex has an independent power sub-station, water system, hospital, police station, banks and other modern facilities. The city boasts of its grand annual event, organized by Gwalior Trade Fair Authority : a nodal agency of Government Of Madhya Pradesh. The event which is a rare and captivating confluence of glorious tradition, culture, tourism and trade with a footfall of 10 million runs for over a month [read more..] The aim of the competition is to redesign Gwalior Trade Fair which can regenerate the allurement of more than 100 year old carnival at the proposed land by Gwalior Trade Fair Authority. Design a sustainable form of Gwalior Vyapaar Mela; blending it with the new technology and architecture which can bring back its old charm through connecting with the emotions of people from all around. A fresh new technique and solution to be adopted to make the proposed space more suitable for different kind of exhibitions. The aim should be to minimize the negative environmental impact of buildings through efficiency and moderation in the use of materials, energy and the ecosystem at large.

Submission Requirements

  • Your proposal must be presented on maximum two landscape oriented A2 sheets using any techniques of your own choice (sketches, diagrams, 3D visualizations, model photos, CAD drawings, etc.)
  • All text must be in English. All delivered material must be marked with your team code, presented clearly in the top right-hand corner of BOTH your sheets. Your sheets must not include ANY information that may give away your identity. Do NOT include your name on your sheets, only the team number.

Format : A single folder (TeamCode.zip) named as your team code containing the following files must be submitted via email to submission@sqrfactor.in

High-resolution PDF containing both A2 sheets for your project. THE MAXIMUM FILE SIZE IS 10MB FOR THIS FILE.

The name of file you shall use is : TeamCode.pdf ,TeamCode(1).jpeg and TeamCode(2).jpeg respectively.

Subject of email : Subject of the email must be your team code.
