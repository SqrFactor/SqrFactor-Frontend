Experienced project Manager

Job Title/Position

Experienced project Manager

Category

Project Manager

Type of position

Full time employee

Skills

AutoCad, Sketch-up and Revit

Educational Qualification

Bachelor of Engineer

Firm

Sqr factor India PVT LTD

Location

India / Madhya Pradesh / Indore

Job expires on

17 / 05 / 2017

Apply through

Jobs@mccleandesign.com

McClean design is a design firm specializing in high end contemporary residences in both Los Angeles and Orange counties. Our projects reflect our interest in the tradition of modernism that is prevalent in southern California. It is a fast-paced environment where we work together as a team in order to handle multiple projects at once. We currently have an expanding work load and are looking for an individual with a strong work ethic and strong modeling and CAD skills.

The successful candidate will be self-motivated, enthusiastic, a diligent worker and a strong team participant to work on a range of residential projects with 5-10 years of relevant office experience. Applicant must be experienced in design development and working drawings, as well as the coordination with consultants and governmental agencies. Knowledge of construction and building codes with extensive experience in production of construction drawings is required. The position will involve the ability to create and develop detailed digital models as well as the ability to visualize and process three-dimensional spaces based upon two-dimensional drawings using both AutoCad, Sketch-up and Revit. Knowledge of Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator along with rendering capabilities will also be required. Entry level applicants should not reply.

The successful applicant must be fluent in English and either a US citizen or permanent resident with excellent written and verbal communication skills. Local applicants only, no relocation will be offered.

Interested applicants should send cover letter, resume and digital portfolio to Jobs@mccleandesign.com.

Package should be no more than 10Mb.

No phone calls.
